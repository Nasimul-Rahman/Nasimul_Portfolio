About Me

Hi! I'm Nasimul. With over 2 years of experience in both front and back-end development, I have the perfect skill set to convert your dream website into reality! Knowing over 7 different programming languages, you may rest assured that all your needs will be met. I can produce Eye-catching and professional portfolios, e-com stores, business sites, non-profit websites, and much more. I strongly believe that communication and collaboration are key to producing the highest quality products, so let's work together in creating websites that are both gorgeous and functional!

I was born and raised in Dhaka Bangladesh, and I've always had a passion for creating all sorts of projects through the web. I am currently studying for a degree in Data Science from The University of Wollongong and have a strong foundation in Web Development.

My academic journey has equipped me with valuable skills such as database management, UX design and networks, which I continually apply in both my personal and professional life. In my professional life, I've had the privilege of working on my own small personal web applications along with a few landing pages for various companies. I'm passionate about all things in tech and strive to make a meaningful impact in my field.
